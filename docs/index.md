## Mission

Performance Paddlesport exists to promote and advocate for human powered paddle sports. Performance Paddlesport strives to foster, promote, and develop participation in sports activities for all ages, with an emphasis on youth, believing these activities provide opportunities for personal development, physical fitness, and teamwork. Performance Paddlesport believes the cultivation of these qualities strengthens communities, encourages responsible citizenship, and contributes to the overall well-being and resilience of society.
