# Performance Paddlesport <br/><small>Excellence through Paddsport</small>

Performance Paddlesport is an organization dedicated to helping paddlers of all levels achieve excellence in their sport. We believe _excellence is about the journey_ of growth and improvement. Performance Paddlesport strives to inspire and support paddlers at every stage of their journey through programs, resources and community. We are passionate about helping paddlers reach their full potential and experience the joy of excellence as a habit.

## Mission

The mission of Performance Paddlesport is fostering the pursuit of excellence as a habit in the medium of human powered paddlesport — not because it is easy, but because the raw power of whitewater is a uniquely honest teacher, a teacher that, through daily, humble practice, instills the confidence and awareness for the pursuit of excellence in any endeavor.
